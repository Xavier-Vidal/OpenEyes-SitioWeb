Open Eyes Project

Free and Open Source Vision

The Open Eyes Project focuses on people with severe visual disabilities. Using free technology and free resources on the net, we managed to design and manufacture a device capable of assisting blind and visually impaired people to improve their quality of life.

Objective

The goal of Open Eyes is to develop a community where free software and hardware are present to improve the lifestyle of people with disabilities.

About Us

Open Eyes is a group of young developers, engineers and anyone else willing to contribute in support of society.

The concern for the visually impaired population stems from the lack of attention to the blind population. In our environment there are many dangers that do not affect people with regular vision, but the visually impaired and blind suffer daily.

Open Eyes is born from that concern, we want to show that with the right aptitude we can mitigate any problem in society, we focus on problems that can be solved through the correct use of technology.

How can I support?

There are many ways to support, you can contribute to the project by reviewing the source code, sharing it on social networks, making a direct donation on PayPal, among other ways. We want everyone to be able to support the project without spending money, so we are updating with new ways to contribute.
